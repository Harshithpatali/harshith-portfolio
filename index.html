I am particularly interested in exploring how Haar wavelets, multi-resolution analysis, and mathematical signal decomposition techniques can enhance modern machine learning systems.

My current exploration focuses on integrating wavelet transforms into deep learning pipelines for:

Financial Time-Series Forecasting ECG Signal Denoising Healthcare AI Transformer Optimization Quantitative AI Systems Hybrid Wavelet-Neural Networks Feature Extraction Deep Learning Compression AI Signal Intelligence Time-Frequency Analysis Biomedical Signal Processing Speech & Audio Recognition

Healthcare & ECG AI
